International High School Education

Flex Major's High School program offers students the opportunity to earn an international diploma. Through a hybrid learning model, students can achieve a Dual Diploma, earning a U.S. high school diploma while simultaneously completing their Brazilian high school. Part of the program is carried out with Flex Major School, providing a bilingual and internationally aligned educational experience.

Who can be a Flex High School student?

  • Minimum level of A2+ or equivalent
  • Proof via Flex Assessment or another international test taken in the past year
  • Students from the 8th year of elementary school onwards

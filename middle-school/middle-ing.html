Middle School

Flex Major School’s Middle School program offers students in lower secondary education the opportunity to earn an American Certificate with international recognition, through the Dual Diploma model.

Students continue their regular studies following the official curriculum of their home country, and those subjects are validated as credits to complement the requirements of the U.S. educational system.

Additional classes provided by Flex Major are delivered online or in a hybrid format, allowing students to complete the subjects required for an American academic pathway.

This results in a bilingual, multicultural, and academically rigorous education, opening doors to top educational opportunities around the globe.

Benefits of being a Flex Middle School student

Dual Certification – Taking advantage of educational equivalences from your country, the student only takes three mandatory credits.

Flexible study pace – Individualized and high-quality study schedule.

Best value for money on the market – Premium quality with affordable investment.

Dynamic and Award-Winning Platform – Complete, interactive, and easy to use for an engaging learning experience.

Elective Courses – Students can explore a complete catalog and expand their knowledge at no additional cost.

Linked academic exchanges – International opportunities to enrich the student's education.

Who Can Join Flex Middle School?

  • Students starting from 5th grade of elementary education
  • ⁠Minimum English level of A1+ (Basic/Intermediate)
  • ⁠Approved through the Flex Assessment or another international test taken within the last year

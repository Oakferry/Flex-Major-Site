College Prep.

What is the program?

Understand the program

College Prep is a short-term exchange program from Flex Major School that offers academic and vocational immersion without compromising the academic year. With full support from planning to return, it provides a unique experience in renowned universities, allowing students to explore their vocation and develop future-ready skills. In addition to improving English fluency and intercultural communication, the program enhances careers with international certification and hands-on experiences. All this under the supervision and reliability of Flex Major School, ensuring a transformative journey full of opportunities.

Join us for a transformative short-term exchange program at Full Sail University in Orlando, Florida, from July 17 to 27, 2025.

Designed for students aged 13 to 17, this experience combines academic learning, vocational exploration, and cultural exchange. Enjoy hands-on workshops, expert-led sessions, and visits to top theme parks like Disney and Universal.

Entry requirements include A2-level English, a valid passport, visa, and authorization to travel. Optional services available for travel assistance.
